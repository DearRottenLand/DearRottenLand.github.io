Description

Dear Rotten Land is a challenging turn-based RPG with dark pixel art aesthetics, set in a medieval era where different types of anthropomorphic animals fight to survive in a decadent world.

Fight all kinds of enemies in combat where the fate of your mercenaries will depend on your strategy and decisions. Find treasures, knowledge and dangers by completing missions throughout the territory.

Get involved in conflicts affecting the different regions, improve your mercenaries and fight the advance of "The Brume" to ensure survival.

Features
  • Handmade pixel art graphics.
  • A new obscure world full of different anthropomorphic animals.
  • Immersive and evolving music.
  • Strategic positional battles, full of traps and challenging enemies.
  • Decisions that will decide the fate of your party.
  • Win against the brume or die trying.
  • Manage and improve mercenaries, equipment, items and skills.
